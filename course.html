Our Courses

Foundation

  • 1 month introductory program for beginners.
  • Methodically designed curriculum to cover all the building blocks of shooting sport.
  • 1 hours of specialized program each day including technical, mental and physical training.
  • Technical and Physical assessment of shooters for ideal selection of discipline (Rifle/Pistol).
  • Complete consumable and equipment support for the entire program

Bull's Eye

  • In-depth elementary training in the discipline of shooter’s choice.
  • 3 months of intensive training program designed for shooters to excel in National and State Level Competitions.
  • 2 hours of specialized program each day including technical, mental and physical training.
  • Consumable and equipment support for the entire program
