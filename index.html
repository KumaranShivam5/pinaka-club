Welcome to PINAKA

About Us

Founded by a dedicated team led by Saurabh Kumar, a distinguished national athlete and accomplished shooter, PINAKA is more than just a rifle shooting club—it's a pathway to excellence. Our mission is to nurture the next generation of national athletes by providing top-notch training and mentorship.

Our Commitment

At PINAKA, we take pride in our commitment to developing talent from the grassroots level. We offer specialized programs designed to train school students and other aspiring athletes, guiding them towards achieving their full potential on the national stage. With a focus on skill development, sportsmanship, and safety, we aim to instill a passion for rifle shooting while nurturing the values of discipline and perseverance.

Join Us

Whether you're a novice eager to learn the fundamentals or a seasoned competitor striving for greatness, PINAKA provides the resources, expertise, and support you need to succeed. Join us as we aim for excellence, with the vision of producing the next generation of national champions.
